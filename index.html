History of the Workshop

The West Coast Plato Workshop was started in 2008 by Rachana Kamtekar and Jan Szaif. They hosted the first meeting at UC Davis, with papers focusing on the text of Theaetetus.

WCPW has been a beloved ancient philosophy workshop ever since, with scholars of all levels, from graduate students to emeritus professors, sharing their interpretations of Plato in a colleagial and sunny (or maybe cloudy, in the years that Pacific Northwest institutions host) environment.

2026 Meeting

The 2026 workshop will be on the text of Republic Books VIII and IX, hosted by Chapman University in Orange, CA.
