Submit

The WCPW invites submissions for the 2026 meeting to be held at Chapman University in Orange, CA, on May 29 - May 31.

Papers addressing any aspect of Books VIII and IX of the Republic, including the relationship of those books to the larger dialogue and to other works, both in Plato's corpus and beyond Plato, are welcome.

Please prepare a 500-word abstract, including the paper's title and at least three keywords, for blind review. Submit prepared abstracts to mcdavid@chapman.edu by December 15, 2025.
